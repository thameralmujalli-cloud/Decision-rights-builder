NOTES FOR THIS MATRIX

    Answer five questions about a specific decision context. The tool returns a recommendation: RACI, RAPID, or both, with the rationale.

    QUESTION 01

    How many people meaningfully influence this decision?

    Influence means their input changes the outcome, not just their awareness.

    QUESTION 02

    How often will this decision type recur?

    Recurrence affects whether you need a heavyweight framework or a lighter one.

    QUESTION 03

    Where is the friction usually?

    Different frameworks solve different friction patterns.

    QUESTION 04

    How clear is the decision question at the start?

    RAPID works best when the question has to be framed before the decision is made.

    QUESTION 05

    What is the cost of a bad call?

    Higher stakes justify more sophisticated frameworks.
