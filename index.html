Coordination Diagnostic

30 items. Six dimensions. One score.

A self-assessment for owner-side project teams. Score your current project across the six dimensions of coordination, identify the priority gaps, and decide where to focus the next 30 days.

Answer 30 items as YES (in place and working), PARTIAL (in place but not fully working), or NO (not in place). The diagnostic returns a percentage score, a per-dimension breakdown, and your top 5 priority gaps to close first.
